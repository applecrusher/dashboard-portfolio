# Public Dashboard Copy

## Title
NYC Vital Signs Monitor: Provisional Birth & Death Trends

## Subtitle
Quarterly provisional NYC birth, maternal-health, infant-mortality, and mortality indicators from NYC Department of Health and Mental Hygiene.

## Short description
An interactive dashboard tracking NYC provisional vital statistics by quarter, population group, borough where available, and cause/category. Use it to spot shifts in births, maternal health indicators, infant mortality, death trends, and leading causes of death.

## Author / publisher
Created by Karl Appel.

## Source attribution
Data source: NYC Department of Health and Mental Hygiene, Provisional Vital Statistics GitHub repository: https://github.com/nychealth/provisional-vital-statistics

## Public caveat / footer
Data are provisional and subject to change, especially recent quarters. Most indicators lag approximately three months. Drug-related death data have a longer lag of approximately six months. Infant mortality rates are smoothed using a four-quarter moving average. Race/ethnicity categories include an “Other/Unknown” group and some populations may be underrepresented due to confidentiality/reporting limitations.
